Fruits Ripeness Detection

Machine learning project


Traditional fruit management methods contribute to wastage and reliance on refrigeration, especially for economically disadvantaged individuals. Our innovation, the "Smart Fruit Basket for Smart Kitchens," integrates IoT sensors and deep learning technology to address this challenge. By employing YOLO optimization, our cost-effective solution monitors multiple fruits' quality in a single basket, detecting ripeness early and reducing waste. Moreover, its real-time capabilities help minimize dependency on refrigeration, offering crucial support for those in need.
